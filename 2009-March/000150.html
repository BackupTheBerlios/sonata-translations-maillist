This is intentional - ctrl-t has different meanings based on the open tab in
sonata. If you press f1 and look at the list of shortcuts, you'll see that
the description for ctrl-t for the current/playlist tabs is "edit selected
song's tags", while for the info tab it is "edit playing song's tags".
