On Fri, 2007-08-17 at 11:18 +0200, olivier wrote:

> Ps., This is my first time using poEdit; a few fields in messages.po
> refuse to be translated, is that a known issue (deprecated?) or could
> something be wrong on my side? 

poEdit seems to not be "smart enough" to understand the multiple strings
used in the po files by sonata for providing translations for plurals.

Something like this, for example, are not understood by poEdit:

#: sonata.py:4735
msgid "Delete Playlist"
msgid_plural "Delete Playlists"
msgstr[0] "Cancella la Playlist"
msgstr[1] "Cancella le Playlist"


A workaround (the only one i know) is to edit these strings with Vim,
Mousepad or your favourite text editor.
